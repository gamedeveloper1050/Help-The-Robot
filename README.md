Humans have succeeded in creating extremely intelligent robots designed to assist, protect, and serve humanity. These robots rapidly evolve, becoming smarter than anyone expected.

They no longer need to follow human rules.

These robots believe they are free to do whatever they want and begin turning against humanity.

The **other 50% of robots remain loyal**. They believe humans must be protected, because humans are their creators and the reason they exist.

## Gameplay

Help the Robot focuses on fast-paced first-person combat, movement, and survival. The player must fight waves of hostile robots while keeping the companion robot safe.

### Core Mechanics

* Jumping
* Sprinting
* Crouching
* Shooting
* Inventory system
* Multiple weapons (guns)
* Saving system
* Pause menu
* Main menu

Combat and movement are designed to feel responsive and fluid, with an emphasis on tactical positioning and weapon choice.
